About r.luvell

Lucian Rodriguez Lovell (he/him) is a Naarm based Researcher and XR Developer.

He studied a Bachelor of Design (Digital Media) at RMIT graduating in 2019. Since then he has been taking on freelance work with local organisations as well as developing a solo body of work using emergent XR technologies.

Lucian is an Industry Fellow (Lecturer) for the RMIT Game Design Program.
